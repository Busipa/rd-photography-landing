About me

Hello!

My name is Renata - I am a wedding, family, and portrait photographer based in Nuremberg, Germany. It is also possible to do photoshoots in other German cities.

I have been enjoying photography for many years. I approach each shoot individually. The creative process during photoshooting - is the main catalyst for my success. I like photography and art ever since I went to art school in Riga. Even then the names of such famous photographers as Ani Leibovitz and Henry Cartier-Bresson were familiar to me. I like to look up to the classics and take the basics of composition from paintings of the 19th century, as well as follow the trends of modern art.

My task is to capture the happy moments of Your life as accurately as possible. I will catch the mood and emotions and leave this moment in Your heart for many years!

My slogan is: “There are no non-photogenic people!. We are all beautiful and I can prove it!"
